Markup

    <svg role="img" class="my-icon">
      <use xlink:href="assets/icons-map.svg#[ICON_ID]"></use>
    </svg>

Browser support

External svgs are not supported by all browsers. For full browser support you can either add icons-map.svg to your DOM, or better yet, use svg4everybody to take care of polyfills.

Svg symbols inherit fill color from their parents, try it out:

#camera

#eyedropper
